Weather App

  • Weather, everybody wants to know how it is going to be during the week. Will it be rainy, windy, or sunny? the app help users to obtain such information

 

App Features 

  • App will detect your location on start and mark it as default location 
  • Default location will always be at the top of the list
  • ability to add as many bookmarks as you want by drag and drop or through search feature
  • Ability to delete bookmarks
  • elegant design
  • Ability to view the next five days broadcast so you can always be ready
  • Ability to change sort order 
  • Ability to change temp unit
  • Ability to see wind speed, humidity and rain chance
